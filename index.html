ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Ulysses A. Tranquilino, a first-year Computer Science student at Ateneo de Naga University. Driven by a passion for technology and its potential to solve real-world problems, I aspire to leverage my skills and knowledge to create innovative solutions. My toolkit includes graphic design, video/photo editing, and problem-solving, which I believe are crucial assets in the ever-evolving field of computer science.

Inspired by Ben Sweetland's quote, "Success is not a destination, it is a journey," I am committed to continuous learning and growth in the field of programming. Beyond my academic pursuits, I find joy in cycling, working out, playing the guitar, designing, video editing, and music. These activities allow me to express my creativity and explore new interests.
